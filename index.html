About

I hold an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Tsinghua University (2021–2025) with a focus on fuel cell systems and control algorithms. My background includes dynamic modeling, control design, and reinforcement learning for physical systems. I have additional training in mechatronics from Beihang University (B.Eng.).

My research centers on modeling and control of power-generating systems, with an emphasis on fuel cell engines and reinforcement-learning-based controllers. I enjoy combining classical control, numerical simulation, and data-driven optimization to solve complex engineering problems.

What I'm Doing

  • Research & Modeling

    Dynamic simulation and control of fuel cell systems; modeling inner states and transport phenomena.

  • Control Algorithms

    Designing MPC, RL-based controllers and numerical approaches for real-time control problems.

  • Simulation & Tools

    MATLAB/Simulink development, optimization algorithms, and multi-domain simulation workflows.
